If you’re planning to travel for more than a couple of weeks, and you don’t have the funds, it’s not a bad idea to consider working or studying abroad, and planning your travel around work and study. Experience abroad, working or studying, looks great on your CV, gives you longer to get to know the country you’re visiting, let’s you make friends from around the world, and is a lot of fun!
